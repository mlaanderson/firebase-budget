About

The Anderson Budget app is a budgetting application for home use. It allows you to put your money to work for you by keeping you on top of your finances.

Features

  • Mobile Friendly
    The web version of the app works equally well on a desktop brower and on a mobile browser.
  • Cloud Based
    The data lives in the cloud, so losing your mobile device or having a hard drive crash does not mean you lose your budget. Changes are stored to the clound almost instantly, and just as quickly are visible to other devices logged into the same account.
  • Easy to Use
    Each pay period you will see where your money is coming from and where it is going.

    If you plan to use an envelope system, transactions marked as cash withdrawals are automatically added up and calculated not only for the right amount of cash to withdraw, but what denominations to request.

    If you mark transactions to be transfered to or from savings, the app will total those up and tell you how much to transfer and whether to transfer the total into or out of your savings account.
  • Secure
    Your data is only available to your login. If you choose to delete your account, your data is immediatly deleted. Your data is only delivered over secure links.
  • Collaborative
    If you share your account with others, they can work collaboratively with you on the budget from another device. You will see their changes and they will see your changes as soon as they are made.
